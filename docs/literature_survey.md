# Literature Survey: Production Risk Analysis & AI Applications

## Contextual Research
Traditional risk management in media production uses manual spreadsheets (like PMBOK-aligned spreadsheets) and qualitative interviews. This approach is slow, static, and fails to adapt to modern agile content creation environments (VFX, fast-paced commercial shoots, and rolling translation tracks).

## Comparative Tool Analysis

| Tool/Platform | Key Strengths | Core Limitations | DigiQuest Solution Gap |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
